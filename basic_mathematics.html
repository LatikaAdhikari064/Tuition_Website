    • A fraction is a way of representing a part of a whole or a ratio between two numbers. It consists of two parts: Numerator: The top number, which represents the number of parts you have or are considering. Denominator: The bottom number, which represents the total number of equal parts in the whole. Fraction = Numerator/ Denominator
